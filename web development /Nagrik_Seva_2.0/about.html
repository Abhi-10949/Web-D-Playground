We are a group of 5 students of chandigarh university committed to overcome challenges faced by labours and customers

Our mission is to...

Connect Labour with Customers, we understand the challenges both labourers and customers face when it comes to finding reliable services or securing jobs. That's why we've created a seamless online platform that bridges the gap between those seeking labour and those offering their skills. Whether you're a homeowner looking for help with a household project or a skilled professional seeking opportunities to showcase your talents, Nagrik Seva is here to make the process effortless and efficient.Our platform offers a diverse range of services, from home repairs and landscaping to tutoring and freelance work. Whatever your needs may be, you'll find experienced and vetted labourers ready to tackle the job with professionalism and dedication.
